How to make grilled cheese sandwiches!

Grilled cheese sandwiches makes the earth go round. There is nothing better than a good, simple grilled cheese sandwiches after a long day of work.

This is a step-by-step instruction on how to create a perfect grilled cheese sandwich.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices white bread
  • Butter
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Generously butter one side of a slice of bread. Place bread butter-side down in the hot skillet; add 1 slice of cheese.

    Butter a second slice of bread on one side and place butter-side up on top of cheese.

  2. Cook until lightly browned on one side; flip over and continue cooking until cheese is melted. Repeat with remaining 2 slices of bread, butter, and slice of cheese.
